Welcome to the Lloyd's Banking Group monthly employee survey!

Your feedback is paramount to us. This survey is designed to help us better understand your experiences, satisfaction, and ideas for improvement in our workplace.

The survey should take about 5–10 minutes to complete. Please answer each question honestly.

Purpose of This Survey

This design is supposed to help us achieve the following:

  • Figuring out the company's strengths in the workplace.
  • A better understanding of how employees feel about company communication
  • Improved transparency between management and employees.
  • A more connected and collaborative workplace.

How satisfied are you with the company's communication channels?

How would you rate the quality of support you get from your supervisors(5 stars being the best help and 1 star being the least help)

On a scale of 1–10, how easy is it for you to reach/access your supervisor?

Thank you for taking our survey! Your feedback helps us improve communication across the company.
