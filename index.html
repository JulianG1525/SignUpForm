Welcome to the Lloyd's Banking Group monthly employee survey!

Your feedback is essential to us. This survey is designed to help us better understand your experiences, satisfaction, and ideas for improvement in our workplace. Please answer each question honestly — your responses will remain confidential and will be used only to improve our organization and work environment.

The survey should take about 5–10 minutes to complete.

Purpose of This Survey

The purpose of this survey is to help us gain a clearer understanding of key areas within our organization and identify meaningful steps toward improvement.It is supposed to help us achieve the following;

  • Figuring out the company's strengths in the workplace.
  • A better understanding of how employees feel about the manager's support
  • Improved transparency between management and employees.
  • A more connected and collaborative workplace.
  • Data-driven plans to improve our overall communication strategy.

How satisfied are you with the company's communication channels?

How would you rate the quality of support you get from your supervisors(5 stars being the best help and 1 star being the least help)

On a scale of 1–10, how easy is it for you to reach/access your supervisor?
